Copyright- The rules an original work has for beinng copied or used.
Copyleft- Similar to copyright, but requires that any modification follows the same rules as the original.
Fair Use- Using something correctly under its copyright rules.
Free Speech- The right usnder the first Amendment 
Free Software- Software that doesn't have to be paid for
Freeware- Same as Free Software?????
Public Domain- When something is open to be used by everyone, usually when the owner or creator is long-dead
File-sharing- Copying a file to someone else without them buying it, usually violates DRM or copyright
Piracy- Copying, ripping, torrenting, etc. something so others don't have to pay for it
Plagiarism- Copying some else's work (usually words) without giving credit
