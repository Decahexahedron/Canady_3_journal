What is pseudocode - What is it good for?
Pseudocode is stuff that looks like code, but isn't actually real code, and it's easier.
More specifically -
Why will we use it?
When we are coming up with the solution to the problem, we will use flowcharts and pseudocode to plan out how to implement a solution.
What about the steps of problem solving?
Part of problem solving involves pseudocode, mostly planning.
